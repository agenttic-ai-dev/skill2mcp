# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on technical arguments, not people.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give actionable feedback.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks.
- Doxxing, threats, or abusive language.
- Repeated disruptive behavior after moderation guidance.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block participation when this code is violated.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately through repository maintainers or platform reporting channels.
